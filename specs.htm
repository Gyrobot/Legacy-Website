Specification

Description

Name

 Gyrobot - From the word Gyrate which is to spin/revolve and to rotate in circles, and erm - robot! Also "Gyro" is short for gyroscope, a device that spins.
Dimensions 700mm diameter x 150mm height.
Weight 98kg.
Construction Steel box section tubular chassis with steel plate and 25mm Polycarb armour.
Weapon 35kg outer spinning steel rim with 4 hardened cutting blades attached. Powered by a 750 watt 24 volt Bosch motor. Weapon timed at 750 RPM. RPM increases to 1500 RPM by simultaneously spinning Gyrobot on the spot.
Power 2 x 16Ah Hawker 12 volt batteries wired in series to give 24 volts.
Drive 2 x 750 watt 24 volt Bosch motors powering chain and sprocket drive.
Speed About 20 mph.
Self Righting Mechanism Can run upside down, no inferior orientation.
Cost £2000 (Currently).
